Form 1040 line flow: Line 1a = total W-2 box 1 wages. Line 9 = total income. Line 11 = adjusted gross income (AGI). Line 12 = standard deduction. Line 15 = taxable income (line 11 minus line 12, not below zero). Line 16 = tax computed on taxable income. Line 22/24 = total tax. Line 25a = federal income tax withheld from W-2 box 2. Line 33 = total payments. If line 33 > line 24, line 34 is the refund; otherwise line 37 is the amount you owe.
