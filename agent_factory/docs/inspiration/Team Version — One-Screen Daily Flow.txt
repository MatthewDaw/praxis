Team Version — One-Screen Daily Flow
Design Goal
✔ Reinforce team culture
✔ Encourage accountability without shaming
✔ Keep individual ownership intact
✔ Strengthen coach & captain leadership
Time to complete: 2–4 minutes
________________


TOP OF SCREEN — Team Identity Anchor
Purpose: reinforce belonging & culture
Display:
 Team Name / Logo
 Today’s Focus Theme
Example:
🏀 Central Eagles Basketball
 Today’s Focus: Next Play Mentality
Optional rotating culture statement:
“We respond. We don’t react.”
Why this matters:
Athletes are reminded: this is who we are.
________________


SECTION 1 — Team Daily Rep (Shared Language)
Purpose: unify mindset focus
Prompt examples:
* “How will you respond when adversity hits today?”

* “What does ‘Next Play’ look like in practice?”

* “How do we show championship effort today?”

Interaction options:
   * Tap response choices

   * 1 short sentence reflection

Completion button:
 ✅ Log My Rep
Athletes see the same focus → shared mindset vocabulary develops.
________________


SECTION 2 — Team Routine Checklist
Purpose: reinforce performance habits
Title: Today’s Team Habits
Example:
      * 🔲 Communicate early & often

      * 🔲 Sprint in transitions

      * 🔲 Encourage a teammate

      * 🔲 Next Play response

Checklist emphasizes behaviors, not outcomes.
Why it works:
Culture becomes visible and actionable.
________________


SECTION 3 — Quick Personal Check-In
Purpose: maintain individual ownership
Ratings (tap or slide):
         * Effort today

         * Focus today

         * Teammate support

This balances personal accountability with team responsibility.
________________


SECTION 4 — Team Consistency Snapshot
Purpose: build accountability without pressure
Display:
            * ✔️ 87% of team completed yesterday

            * 🔥 Team streak: 9 days

            * ↑ Participation trending up

Important:
✔ Show participation
❌ Never show individual rankings
❌ Never shame non-users
This promotes collective ownership.
________________


SECTION 5 — Captain / Coach Message
Purpose: reinforce leadership voice
Rotating message from:
               * Head coach

               * Captains

               * Leadership council

Examples:
                  * “Championship teams communicate when tired.”

                  * “Energy is a choice today.”

                  * “Encourage someone who needs it.”

This builds leadership presence daily.
________________


SECTION 6 — Culture Action of the Day (Optional)
Purpose: reinforce team values through action
Examples:
                     * “Encourage 2 teammates today.”

                     * “Pick someone up after a mistake.”

                     * “Be first to bring energy.”

Simple. Actionable. Repeatable.
________________


FIXED BOTTOM BUTTON
Done for Today
Tap confirms:
✔ Completion
✔ Team participation counted
✔ Streak maintained
________________


What This Screen Builds
CULTURE
Shared language + shared habits
ACCOUNTABILITY
Participation awareness without shame
LEADERSHIP
Captain & coach voice reinforced daily
CONNECTION
Athletes feel part of something bigger
________________

Coach Best Practices for Team Use
Introduce it like this:
“This is how we train our mindset every day — just like strength and conditioning.”
Reinforce weekly:
                        * Mention participation

                        * Reference theme language

                        * Highlight behavior wins

Empower captains:
Have them reference the daily focus in:
                           * warmups

                           * huddles

                           * halftime talks

________________


Coach Truth
Teams don’t build culture in speeches.
They build it through daily shared behaviors.
This screen turns culture from a poster on the wall into something athletes practice every day.
